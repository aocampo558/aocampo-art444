Alyssa was born and raised in Northern California in a small suburbian town called San Ramon, California. She is currently an undergraduate student at San Diego State University majoring in Art with emphasis in Multimedia Design.

She is an artist, writer, videographer, and multi-creative who desires to contribute to society as an artist and make an impact in the world, especially within the environmental and wildlife community. She is passionate about nature and longs to use her skills to help better the world and bring attention to important aspects of the world such as health, wellness, faith, and nature.
